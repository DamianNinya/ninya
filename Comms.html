Common Radio Phrases

  • Roger: Message received and understood.
  • Wilco: Will comply.
  • Over: End of transmission, awaiting response.
  • Out: End of transmission, no response needed.
  • Break Break: Urgent interruption.
  • Say Again: Repeat last transmission.
  • How Copy?: Did you understand?
  • Read You Loud and Clear: Strong and clear transmission.

Tactical Commands

  • Hold: Stay in current position.
  • Move: Advance to next point.
  • Suppress: Provide covering fire.
  • Flank Left/Right: Move to enemy's side.
  • Fallback: Retreat to previous position.
  • Check Your Six: Watch your back.
  • Stack Up: Line up on a doorway for entry.
